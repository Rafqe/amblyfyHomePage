Screen Early. See Clearly.

We solve the challenges parents face when treating children with amblyopia, also known as "lazy eye." Our greatest mission is to create a comprehensive service that will make the treatment journey easier for parents, shorten the healing process, and empower families along the way.

Products for Amblyopia Treatment

We are currently developing applications specifically designed for amblyopia treatment.Our goal is to create an calendar for children, helping both children and parents stay consistent throughout the treatment process — consistency being one of the key factors for successful amblyopia therapy. Additionally, we aim to provide progress tracking through visual acuity tests that can be used between doctor and optometrist visits.Ultimately, we aspire to develop a fun and engaging application that supports amblyopia treatment in a way that is motivating for children.
